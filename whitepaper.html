MASQuerades Whitepaper

Introduction

Welcome to MASQuerades, a groundbreaking card game on the Solana blockchain. This whitepaper outlines our vision to blend strategy, deception, and blockchain technology into an immersive gaming experience.

Game Mechanics

Players build decks from a diverse card library and engage in turn-based battles against an AI opponent, Grok. Cards feature attack, health, and abilities, with combos adding depth. Mana grows each turn, and victory comes by reducing the opponent’s health to zero.

Tokenomics

The $MASQ token drives the game’s economy with a fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. 95.8% is allocated to a public fair launch, ensuring community ownership, while 4.2% is reserved for developers, vested over 4.2 years. There’s no presale—everyone starts equal.

Roadmap

Our journey begins with the game’s launch, followed by new card sets, multiplayer modes, and community events. Future plans include integrating $MASQ for in-game rewards and expanding to other blockchains.

Team

Developed by an anonymous collective of blockchain experts and game designers, united by a passion for creating a fair, engaging experience under the MASQ.
